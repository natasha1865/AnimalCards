Tyrannosaurus Rex

The species Tyrannosaurus Rex was one of the largest land carnivores of all time. Rex meaning "king" in Latin, often called T-Rex.

  • Killer Instinct : 10
  • Length : 12 Meters
  • Height : 4 Meters
  • Weight : 7000kgs

T-Rex was a large, predatory dinosaur. It stood on two powerful hind legs, and had a muscular body and tail. Its arms, were tiny, with two claws at each end. Its massive head, and strong jaws were filled with 54 -60 long, sharp teeth.
